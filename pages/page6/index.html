ICHIGO KUROSAKI


Ichigo Kurosaki: Once a highschool student, turned into a shinigami. He would soon become one of the strongest shinigami in history.

SOSHIRO HOSHINA


Soshiro Hoshina: Vice Captain of the Third Division. Also known for his small kaiju elimination skills.

HINATA SHOYO


Hinata Shoyo: A passionate player that entered Karasuno in his Highschool days. He would then soon become a professional volleyball player for the MSBY Black Jackals

JETT


Jett: A VALORANT agent who specializes in mobility and assassinations. Like a true ninja, this South Korean agent can dash short distances to catch-up enemies or escape dangerous situation, and even propel herself upwards to reach highground places and surprise lurking targets who think they're safe up there.

SHADOW FIEND


Shadow Fiend: Also known as nevermore. He is feared even among the demons because of his power to collect souls.
